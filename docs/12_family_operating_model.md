# Naam - Family Operating Model

## Version

v1.0

---

# Philosophy

Families are organizations.

Like every organization, families continuously receive information, make decisions, assign work, execute responsibilities and preserve knowledge.

Most families perform these activities mentally.

Naam exists to make this operating model visible, organized and collaborative.

# Long-Term Vision

Naam becomes the trusted operational memory of the family.

Rather than replacing conversations, calendars or messaging applications, Naam quietly coordinates the work required to keep family life running smoothly.

Families should spend less time remembering and coordinating, and more time living together.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every feature added to Naam must answer one question:

"Does this reduce the mental load of running a family?"
